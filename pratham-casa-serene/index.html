About

Pratham Constructions its latest illustrious project - Casa Serene! Located at Jalahalli in Bangalore, Casa Serene is meticulously designed residential gated community. Consisting of 140 spacious apartments, Casa Serene offers premium 2 and 3 BHKs to choose from. Designed to befit ultra-modern lifestyles of the modern Indian families, at Casa Serene experience magnificent ambience of luscious greenery combined with ultra-modern amenities for a modern and contemporary lifestyle.

The highlights of amenities include swimming pool, club house, badminton court, Car wash area, Jogger’s track, high-speed elevators, 24/7 surveillance camera, 100% DG Back up for common areas, clutter free provision to connect DTH of your choice, water treatment plant-advanced, soft and grey, sewage treatment plant, solar water heater, solid waste convertor, rain water harvesting, connectivity, vehicle charging bays, car wash bays, round the clock security, restrooms for support staff, maintenance office cabin, disabled friendly community, sensor and timer controlled LED Lights for entire common area lighting.
